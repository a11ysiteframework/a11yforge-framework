About A11yForge

A11yForge was created to make accessibility and mobile-first usability a priority from the start. Unlike other frameworks, it is lightweight, dependency-free, and built with inclusivity in mind. The goal is to empower developers to build projects that everyone can use, regardless of ability or device.
